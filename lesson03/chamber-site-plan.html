Recife's Chamber Site Plan

Site name

Recife Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Recife Chamber of Commerce stands as the entity which supports the business interests of their members, giving voice to their associates demands. We promote events, conferences and meetings with governamental agencies in order to improve the conditions of employers and their employees.Besides, We act along with local congressmen in formulating policies to generate jobs and prosperity in Recife City.

The Chamber of Commerce will be promoting a local entrepreneurs and the society meeting in order to raise funds to help a public hospital in the city. This event will count with mayor's presence, congressmen, business people, intellectuals, and non-governamental organizations.

You can contact our board members through either social media or emailing us using the Contact us section.

The interest in the area as increased a lot over the last five years.
